### [Divalent Cation-Mediated Polyanion Attraction in Aqueous Solution](https://meetings.aps.org/Meeting/MAR23/Session/B15.1)
<span class="research-badge research-badge--physics">APS March Meeting · 2023</span>

We clarify the mechanism through which multi-valent ions result in polyelectrolyte precipitation and present design principles for polyelectrolytes with higher calcium tolerance values. Uses well-tempered metadynamics and Hamiltonian replica exchange to calculate two-chain potentials of mean force as a function of ionic strength, temperature, and functional group identity.

**Venue:** American Physical Society March Meeting · Las Vegas, NV · March 6, 2023
