### [Divalent Cation-Mediated Polyanion Attraction in Aqueous Solution](https://meetings.aps.org/Meeting/MAR23/Session/B15.1)

<span class="research-badge research-badge--physics">APS March Meeting · 2023</span>

Explains why calcium and other multivalent ions cause charged polymers to precipitate out of solution, and proposes design rules for polymers that resist this failure mode. Applies enhanced-sampling molecular dynamics to compute the effective interaction between polymer chains across varying salt concentration, temperature, and monomer chemistry.

**Venue:** American Physical Society March Meeting · Las Vegas, NV · March 6, 2023

[Abstract](https://meetings.aps.org/Meeting/MAR23/Session/B15.1)
