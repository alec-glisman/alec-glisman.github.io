### Polyelectrolyte Complexation & Ion Binding

Calculated adsorption isotherms and free-energy landscapes for Ca²⁺ binding on poly(acrylic acid) using well-tempered metadynamics and Hamiltonian replica exchange. Mapped concentration-dependent phase diagrams revealing how multivalent ions drive polyelectrolyte chain association and precipitation. Developed generative diffusion models for polymer sequence design with enhanced calcium tolerance. Collaborated with Dow Chemical on water treatment applications.

**Key papers:** [Langmuir 2025](/publication/2025-langmuir-binding-modes) · [Macromolecules 2024](/publication/2024-macromolecules-polyelectrolyte) · [Langmuir 2024](/publication/2024-langmuir-adsorption-isotherm)

### Microhydrodynamics & Active Matter

Derived analytical framework showing self-propulsion mechanics are identical across Reynolds number regimes: both Stokes swimmers and potential flow swimmers exploit configuration-dependent hydrodynamic resistance and added mass. Developed C++/CUDA multi-threaded boundary integral simulations for many-particle hydrodynamic interactions. Demonstrated how purely mechanical coupling drives collective emergent structures.

**Key paper:** [J. Fluid Mechanics 2022](/publication/2022-potential-flow-2)

### Lipid Membrane Mechanics

Introduced the Scriven-Love number — a dimensionless ratio quantifying when intramembrane viscosity significantly affects membrane relaxation dynamics. Developed continuum theory coupling in-plane viscous flow to out-of-plane elastic bending using differential geometry and linear irreversible thermodynamics. Demonstrated the Scriven-Love number spans physiologically relevant ranges across diverse biological processes, indicating in-plane viscous forces cannot be neglected in membrane analysis.

**Key paper:** [Physical Review E 2020](/publication/2020-scriven-love-1)
